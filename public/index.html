FAQ
Is there a free trial? +
No trial needed — the Free plan is free forever. Build and publish one site with 50 AI messages a day, and upgrade to Pro only when you need more.
Can I switch plans or cancel later? +
Yes. Upgrade or cancel anytime from your account — billing is handled through the Stripe customer portal, and your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? +
All major credit cards, processed securely by Stripe.
What is your refund policy? +
Refunds are handled case-by-case — reach out to support@tribox.app and we'll make it right.
